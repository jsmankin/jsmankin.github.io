Postdoctoral Research


There are a few postdoc possibilities to work with our group:


1. PROF Provost's Postdoctoral Fellow in interdisciplinary Climate Science

You can apply to work with the group through this prestige fellowship with a cohort of scholars at Dartmouth. See the Interfolio application here or email Justin to discuss.


2. The Neukom Postdoctoral Research Fellowship

This is an interdisciplinary computational postdoctoral fellowship sponsored by the Neukom Institute, based around an original proposal. If you have an interest in applying and being a postdoc in our group, this is one avenue to do so. Applications completed by November 15 receive full consideration. Email Justin to discuss.


3. Postdoctoral Fellow in Mortality Impacts of Heat Waves

You can apply directly to work with the group in a collaboration between CMIG and Professor Klaus Keller's research group. See the Interfolio application here or email Justin to discuss.

Graduate Student Research


Graduate students can join the lab via two pathways pending funding, EEES and Earth Sciences. Please email Justin briefly stating your research interests and CV if you'd like to apply.



1. The Ecology, Evolution, Ecosystems & Society (EEES) PhD program at Dartmouth College. See our group advertisement here. (The deadline is December 1, though it is somewhat flexible.)



2. The Department of Earth Sciences (EARS) at Dartmouth College. (Deadline January 15th.)



Undergraduate Research


If you are a Dartmouth undergraduate student, a geography department honors thesis student, or interested in working with the group on climate research send Justin an email briefly outlining your research interests and we can set up a time to discuss research projects.
